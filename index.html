*GAME ABOUT CLIMBING*

version 1.1; 24/11/2023

Main goal is to get to the top of Triglav, slovenian highest peak. There are many routes one can use to ascend to Triglav, but I will focus only on the climbing ones. But there is also a lot of other peaks in Julian Alps a novice can try before Triglav.

CLIMBING AREAS

The routes will be taken from https://www.bergsteigen.com/ and adapted for the needs of this project.

climbing_section

strength and technique and protection_placing difficulities

strength, technique, protection_placing (whether a character is able to place a protection e.g. see a crack, nest, hourglasses... test against characteristic of a climbing_section) health, tiredness (preparation for future) rope_management (preparation for future; will affect HP loss when fall occurs and stamina loss while climbing multipitch)

INVENTORY: climbing guide (there are information about the routes) & other stuff

version 1.0; 19/10/2023

The idea is to enhance the game I made few years ago.

CHARACTERS

There will be 2 characters with abilities (strength, technique and rope management). They are going to have an inventory. In that invenotry there would (or would not) be [pitons & hammer], [camalots], [wedges], [carabiners], [climbing guide].

MAP

There will be a Lobby from which you can proceed to the Shop or Climbing areas. In Shop you can buy [pitons & hammer], [camalots], [wedges], [carabiners], [climbing guide]. Many routes are going to be in Climbing areas with different difficulty, length, protection possibilities, ...
